Privacy Policy for ChatGPT Pro Tool

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Overview

ChatGPT Pro Tool ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Usage

What Data We Collect

The Extension collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Saved Prompts: Text prompts that you create and save within the extension
    • Prompt titles
    • Prompt content
    • Creation timestamps

How We Use Your Data

  • Prompt Storage: Your saved prompts are stored locally in Chrome's storage API to provide quick access to your frequently used prompts
  • Prompt Insertion: When you use a prompt, the extension inserts the text into the ChatGPT interface on your behalf

Data Storage Location

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. We do not:

  • Send your data to any external servers
  • Upload your data to the cloud
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage or behavior

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save your custom prompts locally on your device
  • Data Access: Only data you explicitly create within the extension

Active Tab Permission

  • Purpose: To insert prompts into the ChatGPT interface when you click "Use Prompt"
  • Data Access: Only when you actively use the extension on ChatGPT pages

Scripting Permission

  • Purpose: To add scroll controls and enable prompt insertion functionality on ChatGPT pages
  • Data Access: Limited to ChatGPT domains (chatgpt.com and chat.openai.com)

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. All functionality operates entirely within your browser.

Data Retention

Your saved prompts remain stored locally on your device until you:

  • Manually delete them through the extension interface
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser's extension data

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The Extension is designed for general use and does not target children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API, your data security is managed by Chrome's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using strong device passwords
  • Being cautious about who has access to your device

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your saved prompts within the extension
  • Modify: Edit any saved prompt
  • Delete: Remove individual prompts or uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • Export: Copy prompt content for backup purposes

Compliance

This Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
